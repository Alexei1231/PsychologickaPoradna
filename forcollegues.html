At THERAPY s.r.o., we are dedicated to providing comprehensive psychological support tailored to adolescents, teens, and women from all walks of life. Our mission is to create a safe, supportive, and empowering environment where clients can navigate their challenges and achieve personal growth.

Our Achievements

  • Successfully supported over over 500 clients through evidence-based therapeutic interventions.
  • Established partnerships with numerous community organisations, providing outreach and education on mental health awareness.
  • Recognized as a leading center for adolescent and women's mental health support in the Czech Republic.
  • Conducted numerous workshops and seminars on stress management, self-esteem, and coping strategies for diverse audiences.
  • Developed a scholarship program for underprivileged clients, ensuring accessibility to quality mental health care.
  • Published articles and research in different journals focusing on mental health in adolescents and women.

Our Methods

We utilize a diverse array of psychological approaches to meet the unique needs of each individual:
  • Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT): To address thought patterns and behaviors impacting mental health.
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT): For clients dealing with emotional regulation and interpersonal challenges.
  • Mindfulness and Stress Reduction Techniques: To help clients build resilience and manage anxiety.
  • Family Therapy: To resolve conflicts and foster healthy dynamics within family systems.
  • Trauma-Informed Care: Ensuring safety and understanding for clients with past trauma.
  • Art and Play Therapy: Especially effective for adolescents and younger teens in expressing emotions.
  • Group Therapy: Building community and shared understanding among clients.
  • Solution-Focused Brief Therapy: Concentrating on immediate goals and actionable strategies.
  • At the heart of our work is a commitment to inclusivity, empathy, and evidence-based practices, ensuring every client receives personalized and impactful care. We invite our colleagues to join us in fostering a brighter future for those we serve.
