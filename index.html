Introduction

Mgr. Dmytro Meľnyk, Ph.D. is a child and adolescent psychologist specializing in emotional and behavioral issues in children and teenagers. His work focuses on promoting mental well-being, stress management, social skills development, and resolving family conflicts. With over 10 years of experience in psychological counseling, Dr. Meľnyk offers both individual and group therapy. He is known for his empathetic approach and the use of modern therapeutic techniques, including cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT).

Mgr. et Bc. Marie Nováková provides dedicated support for women facing mental health challenges. She focuses on issues such as postpartum depression, anxiety, and emotional well-being. With over 15 years of experience and a compassionate, empathetic, women-centered approach, she helps clients regain balance and confidence in their lives.

Our goals and values

Our goal is to empower individuals on their journey toward mental well-being, personal growth, and resilience. We are committed to creating a compassionate, safe, and non-judgmental environment where clients can explore their thoughts and emotions. Guided by values of empathy, integrity, and respect, we tailor our therapeutic approach to meet each person's unique needs. Our mission is to foster self-awareness, encourage positive change, and help clients build the tools they need to thrive in all areas of life. Your well-being is at the heart of everything we do.
