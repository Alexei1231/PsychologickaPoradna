Mgr. Dmytro Meľnyk, Ph.D. is a highly experienced child and adolescent psychologist with over 10 years of dedicated practice. He specializes in supporting young people through critical stages of emotional, cognitive, and social development. Dr. Meľnyk's expertise extends beyond clinical practice; he has conducted extensive research on teenagers' mental development across various cultural contexts, providing him with a global perspective on youth psychology.
Throughout his career, Dr. Meľnyk has successfully helped more than 300 children and adolescents achieve emotional well-being and resilience, earning the trust and gratitude of families. His compassionate, evidence-based approach integrates cultural sensitivity and individualized care, making him a trusted expert in promoting mental health and growth in young people.
